Loan Assessment Fields

Credit Grade

A standardized rating system (0-7) that evaluates your creditworthiness based on comprehensive financial history. This grade directly influences loan terms and interest rates.

Critical

Borrower APR

The comprehensive Annual Percentage Rate that encompasses all loan costs, including interest and associated fees. This metric provides a standardized measure of the total cost of borrowing.

High

Borrower Rate

The base interest rate applied to your loan principal. This rate is determined by your credit profile and market conditions, forming the foundation of your loan's cost structure.

High

Lender Yield

The net return on investment for lenders after accounting for service fees. This metric is crucial for determining the attractiveness of your loan to potential investors.

Medium

Prosper Score

A proprietary risk assessment metric (1-10) developed through analysis of historical loan performance data. This score provides a comprehensive evaluation of repayment probability.

Critical

Credit Score

A standardized numerical representation of your creditworthiness, derived from your credit history. This score is a fundamental component in risk assessment and loan pricing.

Critical

Monthly Loan Payment

The calculated monthly obligation required to service your loan, including principal and interest components. This figure is essential for debt service coverage analysis.

High

Customer Payments

The aggregate of all payments made toward your loan, encompassing both principal and interest components. This metric reflects your payment history and reliability.

Medium

Interest and Fees

The cumulative amount of interest and associated fees paid on your loan. This metric provides insight into the total cost of borrowing over the loan term.

Medium

Service Fees

Administrative charges associated with loan servicing and management. These fees cover the operational costs of maintaining your loan account.

Low

Principal Payments

The portion of your payments that reduces the original loan amount. This metric tracks the progress of principal reduction over the loan term.

Medium

Collection Fees

Charges incurred for managing delinquent accounts and late payments. These fees are only applicable in cases of payment default or delinquency.

Low

Gross Principal Loss

The total amount of loan principal written off due to defaults. This metric is crucial for risk assessment and portfolio management.

Medium

Loan Original Amount

The initial principal amount of your loan before any interest or fees. This figure represents the base amount of credit extended to you.

High

Stated Monthly Income

Your declared monthly income, which is a critical factor in determining debt service capacity and loan eligibility. This metric is essential for affordability assessment.

Critical
